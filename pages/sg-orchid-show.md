![SG Orchid Show](/images/sg-orchid-show.jpg "SG Orchid Show 2019")

A spinoff from the main show, organised in partnership with the Orchid Society of South East Asia. Orchid landscape displays and award-winning orchid plants will be showcased at the National Orchid Garden and other parts of the Singapore Botanic Gardens. Talks, demonstrations and guided tours will also further acquaint visitors with this flower that is closely linked to Singapore’s identity, while a marketplace will feature a myriad of plant, gardening and landscape-related products for sale.
