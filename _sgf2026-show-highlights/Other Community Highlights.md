<h4>Penjing and Artistic Stone Exhibition and Competition 2026&nbsp;</h4>
<p>Organised by the Singapore Penjing and Stone Appreciation Society, this
event showcases tropical bonsai species, including mature specimens of
up to a century old, and a range of artistic and landscape stones.</p>
<h4>Singapore Gardeners Cup</h4>
<p>First introduced in 2010, this friendly competition for local community
gardeners celebrates our flourishing gardening culture in Singapore, and
serves as a platform to share, deepen knowledge of plants, horticultural
skills, landscape implantation expertise and new design ideas.</p>
