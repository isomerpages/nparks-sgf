<p>The Floral Designers Society (Singapore) is proud and honored to present
the 14th International FDSS Cup – Floral Designing Competition, held in
conjunction with the Singapore Garden Festival 2026.</p>
<p>The theme for the 2026 edition of the FDSS Cup centres on “The Elements
of Design.” Upholding its strong commitment to floral education and excellence,
FDSS continues to champion the artistry and professionalism of floral design.
Each competition task will showcase and celebrate the fundamental elements
that define exceptional floral artistry.</p>
<p>This year, the much-anticipated “LIVE” competition returns with two days
of excitement. The Amateur Category will take place on Saturday, 4 July
2026, while the Professional Category will be held on Wednesday, 8 July
2026, both at Gardens by the Bay – The Meadow. Competition pieces will
be on public display daily from 4 to 12 July 2026, until 10:00 pm.</p>
<p>Both local and international floral designers, subject to specific entry
requirements are welcome to register by 31 March 2026, 2359 (Singapore
Time).</p>
<p>All participants will have the distinguished honour of having their creations
evaluated by an esteemed panel of international floral design judges and
the top three winners will receive attractive cash prizes, trophies, and
certificates of recognition.</p>
