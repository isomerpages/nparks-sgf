<p>The Show Gardens competition is a hallmark event of the Singapore Garden
Festival (SGF), featuring garden displays by award-winning local and international
garden and landscape designers. It debuted at the first edition of SGF
in 2006, and has been a mainstay at every subsequent edition of SGF since.&nbsp;</p>
<p>With SGF marking its milestone 10th edition in 2026, the iconic competition
will return with an All-Stars edition, featuring eight previous “Best of
Show” winners vying for the prestigious Pinnacle Award.</p>
<p>This year’s Show Gardens: All-Stars Edition will be held this July at
Gardens by the Bay, where designers will present gardens that reflect their
creativity, styles and cultural influences, with support from local implementing
partners.&nbsp;Designers will be judged based on their application of horticultural
design principles, planting concepts and the quality of plants showcased.</p>
