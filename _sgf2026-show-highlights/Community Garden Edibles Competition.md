<h5>CGEC 2026: A Decade of Growing Excellence!</h5>
<p>Launched in 2015, the Community Garden Edibles Competition (CGEC) 2026
recognises the abilities of our local gardeners and showcases their excellent
harvests, including uncommon and unique edibles.</p>
<p>Participants, be it an individual or together as a gardening group, can
choose from 10 diverse 'Best Edibles' categories plus an Open Category
to send in their harvest(s), showcasing the remarkable varieties of fruits
and vegetables that can flourish in our urban environment.</p>
<p>Registration is open from <strong>12 January 2026 to 13 May 2026.</strong>
</p>
<p>To find out more information about the competition and how to register,
click <a href="https://gardeningsg.nparks.gov.sg/community-garden-edibles-competition/" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a>.</p>
