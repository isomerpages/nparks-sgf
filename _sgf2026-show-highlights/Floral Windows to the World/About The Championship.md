<p>Floral Windows to the World has been a key highlight of the Singapore
Garden Festival since 2006, showcasing colourful and creative floral displays.
Following the successful introduction of the Championship format in 2024,
this live competition returns with 17 international floral designers competing
for the title of Grand Champion.</p>
<p>The Floral Windows to the World Championship (FWWC) will take place over
3 days at The Meadow at Gardens by the Bay, featuring six elimination rounds
that narrow the field from 17 competitors to the final 5 contenders. Visitors
can watch the designers create their works under pressure, witnessing the
creative process unfold and observing the skill required to excel in competitive
conditions from 4 to 6 July 2026.</p>
<p>The Championship concludes at the Finals dinner, where the top 5 competitors
face their ultimate challenge and 1 Grand Champion will be crowned.&nbsp;</p>

up close while enjoying dinner. This ticketed event offers the only opportunity
to experience the climactic showdown firsthand. Your dinner ticket includes

entire festival experience.
