![SGF Hort Show](/images/sgf-hort-show.jpg "SGF Hort Show")

The first-ever SGF Horticulture Show will be happening from 27 April to 5 May 2019 at Jurong Lake Gardens! This offshoot of the popular Singapore Garden Festival (SGF) will showcase horticulture excellence with local industry partners, students and the community demonstrating their talent and expertise.

Click [here](/files/SGF-hort-show-2019-show-map.pdf) to download Show Map.

Key highlights:

- **Horticultural Competition (27 April - 1 May)** comprising the Best Show Plants across nine categories cultivated by professionals and amateurs vying for Prestigious Awards, Medals and the Grand Prize, The City in a Garden Cup!
Click here to download the [poster](/images/hc2019-poster.jpg) / [competition kit](/files/hort-comp-2019-schedule.pdf) / [plant submission shuttle bus](/files/plant-submission-shuttle-bus.pdf) / [winners' list](/files/hort-comp-winners-list.pdf)

- **Horticultural Display (2 May - 5 May)** showcasing uncommon, unusual and unique plants in the National Parks Board's Pasir Panjang Nursery collection, which will be sure to excite all gardening enthusiasts! 

- **Vertical Greenery Competition** featuring unique creations of 36 square metres each designed by professionals from Singapore’s landscape industry based on the theme of “Into the next dimension”
Click [here](/files/vgc-winnders-list.pdf) to download the winners' list

- **Aquascape Display including the Nano Tank Aquascaping Competition** where visitors can feast their eyes on beautiful underwater garden landscapes 
Click [here](/files/ad-comp-kit.pdf) to download the competition kit

- **My Mini Eden** where a collection of creative terrariums by students and the community will demonstrate how gardening can be done indoors within a small space – even the display area will be a larger-than-life terrarium!

- **Magical World of Miniature Settings** in which local talents from NParks and various Institutes of Higher Learning will tell a myriad of stories through scaled dioramas filled with miniature landscapes and props

- **Beautiful Bonsai!**, an exhibition of exquisite specimens from Jurong Lake Gardens, local bonsai collectors and nurseries

- The popular **MarketPlace** offering plants, gardening and horticultural products, as well as food and drinks
